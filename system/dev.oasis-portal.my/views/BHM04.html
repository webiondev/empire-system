The hospitality and tourism industry encompasses (Includes) lodging, travel and tourism, recreation, amusements, attractions, resorts, restaurants, and food beverage service. The hospitality and tourism industry also maintains the largest national employment base in the private sector. Tourism Is the activities of people traveling to and staying in places outside their usual environment for leisure, business or other purposes for not more than one consecutive year. Hospitality refers to the relationship between a guest and a host, wherein the host receives the guest with goodwill, including the reception and entertainment of guests, visitors, or strangers.
