It is the application of the principles of food and beverage management in full service restaurants existing as independent units or as units within a commercial/non commercial food service operation.

Students will have an understanding of restaurant and kitchen system.

The student will:

Understand the strength and weakeness in business & management

Understand the importance financial planning and cost control.

Understand the process of kitchen operation.
