Tour planning and operation is Structure of tour operator business, related departments, destination survey for producing itinerary, selecting accommodation, transfers and transportation, managing meals and activities, controlling guides and training, promoting, costing and quotation, marketing, launching and operating the tour.

Students will learn the history and types of Tour we have in the industry

Students will learn the about the Importance of Tour Operator as well as the meaning, Scope and functions as well.

Students will learn the different types of Tour Operator

Students will learn to Identify various important national and international tour operators.

The student will:

Understand the history and types of Tour we have in the industry

Understand about the Importance of Tour Operator as well as the meaning, Scope and functions as well.

Understand the different types of Tour Operator

Understand how to Identify various important national and international tour operators.
