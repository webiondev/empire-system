This guide explains how to use the booking system for One-to-One consultations, which are part of NECâ€™s educational programme. This system is used to help to schedule lecturer's consultation hours. Students can make appointments with their lecturer via this system using different communication media sources.

Consultation Hours tab located at the Course Details section of the menu. You are able to book a new consultation slot or view your bookings.

Book new slot

The screen is split into two sections: Your details at the top and the list of currently available appointments.

In order to check available slots, you need to select a module for your consultation. If lecturer created slots for the week, they will be available on the screen, as shown on the image above.

Second step is selecting a communication channel. Once you decide which channel is more suitable for you, please specify your channel ID e.g Skype ID in appointment reason field, in order for the lecturer to contact you.

If there are no consultation slots available, you may contact with the lecturer using email address provided on the screen.

View Bookings Made

Once you make your booking, you can check the status on the "View Consultations Made" page. If booking is approved, that means the lecturer will be waiting for you at the chosen time for a further discussion using your communication source.
