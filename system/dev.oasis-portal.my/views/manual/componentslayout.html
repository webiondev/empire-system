This guideline is designed to describe the system layout, in order to familiarize you with systems components, and to ease your educational process.

Once you login into your account, and have an access to your course, you will see a learning management system dashboard.

Top Navigation bar

On the right top corner you can see the list of icons that you need to become familliar with.

First icon from the left is a user manual icon. If you will have any difficulties with a system usage or education process, you can always go through the manual. Second icon is a account settings page. If you want to change a profile picture or your account password, you can do it on this page. Next icon is a system notifications panel. Everytime there is a system changes or any educational updates such as for e.g new uploaded materials you will be notified right away. The last icon is your account overview and Logout.

Right after the navigational icons, there is a right sidebar trigger on a top right corner. The sidebar is displaying your pending tasks, include an online communication channel between students and lecturers as long as your personal progress.

Left Sidebar menu

On the left side of the page, there is a sidebar menu, which is designed to make you navigational process easier, and allows you to access all system functionality.

Course and Academic information includes detailed information about New England College as well as your course syllabus.

Modules tab provides you an access to your module materials such as slides, assignment question, notes, video materials. The next section is assignment submission module which is designed for posting your coursework for the further evaluation by lecturer.

Tasks tab provide you the list of your current tasks, that has to be completed before the chosen due date.

Consultation Hours system allows you to communicate with a lecturers for solving your personal educational matters.

Web Enrollment System is displaying your current or past progress during your studies

Course Fee Details shows your current fee statement.

Examination and Test systems will be used for evaluating your knowledges obtained during your self-studies.

Settings page allows you to edit your personal account information.

Dashboard

Dashboard contain a sections such as: User Info, Announcements, Task widget and Quick Navigational buttons.

User Info displays you profile picture, current semester and number of modules in a semester. Announcement section is used by lecturers to notify you about results, examinations and test dates and access codes, assignments and so on.

Task widget displays your current tasks. Quick Navigational buttons ease your navigation to the system components.
