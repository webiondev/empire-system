Teaching in the Campus will take various forms as outlined below. Each module is structured around 150 total study hours.

Materials provided by the Module Leader will be available in the Campus and will follow a logical process in preparation for the assessment and cover areas related to each Learning Outcome for the module.

Any additional details and guidance required will be provided by the Module Leader.

  • Students must regularly attend and participate in all of the online events as directed by the Module Leader.
  • Regular effective discussion forum participation from the students.
  • Students may seek regular contact and feedback from the Module Leader on their own individual progress.
  • Self-evaluation of previous test performance, and work on any areas of development by each student.
  • Individual revision and research throughout the programme.
  • Student-managed revision sessions.

The student is reminded that live, synchronous discussion forum ('live chat') content should not be regarded as exhaustive and that for each Module Leader directed activity the student is expected to carry out substantive self-managed independent study.
