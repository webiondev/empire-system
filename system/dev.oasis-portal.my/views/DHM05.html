Convention management is one of the hospitality industry’s fastest growing occupations. Convention managers are responsible for coordinating services for a variety of gatherings, including: business seminars, athletic expositions, educational workshops and entertainment functions. These events cover all aspects of hospitality, including booking rooms, providing food services, and setting up the venues for activities. Managing these events proficiently is a huge factor in providing satisfactory experiences to participants and encouraging them to return in the future to rebook similar engagements.
